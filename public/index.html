Studio Ghibli API

Supported Endpoints & Methods:

  • Films

  • GET All Films (/films)

    fetch('http://api-ghibli.herokuapp.com/films')

  • GET One Film (/films/:id)

    fetch('http://api-ghibli.herokuapp.com/films/12cfb892-aac0-4c5b-94af-521852e46d6a')

  • People

  • GET All People (/people)

    fetch('http://api-ghibli.herokuapp.com/people')
